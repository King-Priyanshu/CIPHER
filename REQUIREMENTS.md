# Project Requirements

## User Registration & Login
- Secure user registration & login
- Individual user profiles (mandatory)
- Terms & Conditions checkbox on signup/login
- Consent for automatic recurring payment deduction
- Password recovery & account security
- **Role-based access**:
    - Admin
    - Subscriber
    - Guest

## Subscription System
- Monthly / Quarterly / Annual plans
- Optional free trial or entry-level plan
- Auto-renewal with manual cancellation option
- Upgrade / downgrade plans anytime
- Pause or cancel subscription
- Clear billing & invoice history

## User Dashboard (Subscriber Side)
- Subscription status & plan management
- Billing & payment history
- Active & upcoming projects
- Reward tracking & distribution history
- Notifications & important updates

## Admin Dashboard (Platform Control)
- User & subscriber management
- Subscription plans & pricing control
- Project creation & management
- Fund allocation tracking
- Revenue & reward distribution control
- Coupons, discounts & referral management
- Content & announcement management

## Payments & Billing
- Secure payment gateway integration
- Recurring auto-deduction support
- Invoice & receipt generation
- Failed payment handling & retry logic
- Tax / GST configuration (if applicable)

## Design & User Experience
- Clean, modern, and premium UI
- **Focus on**:
    - Trust
    - Transparency
    - Simplicity
    - Community confidence
- Fully responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop

## Security & Compliance
- SSL & secure authentication
- Encrypted sensitive data
- Role-based access control
- Regular backups
- Anti-fraud & bot protection
- Compliance-friendly structure

## SEO & Performance
- SEO-friendly structure
- Fast page load & optimized assets
- Sitemap & indexing setup
- Analytics integration (as required)

## Deliverables
- Fully functional subscription-based website
- Admin panel with full access
- Source code & documentation
- Basic admin usage guidance
- Deployment & go-live support

## Out of Scope
- Content creation (unless provided)
- Marketing or ad campaigns
- Mobile app (planned for future phase)
