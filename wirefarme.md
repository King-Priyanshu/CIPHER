## 1Ô∏è‚É£ Overall Working Flow (Simple Language)

**Step 1: User Joins**
A user visits the CIPHER website, understands the concept, and signs up.

**Step 2: Subscription**
User selects a subscription plan (monthly / quarterly / yearly) and enables auto-payment.

**Step 3: Fund Pool Creation**
All subscriber payments go into a **common subscription pool**.

**Step 4: Project Selection**
The platform (admin / committee):

* Selects trusted third-party projects **OR**
* Creates internal community projects

**Step 5: Fund Allocation**
Money from the pool is allocated to these projects.

**Step 6: Project Execution**
Projects run, generate revenue or value.

**Step 7: Reward Pool**
Generated profit/value is moved into a **Reward Pool**.

**Step 8: Reward Distribution**
Rewards are distributed to subscribers based on:

* Subscription plan
* Duration
* Contribution rules

**Step 9: Transparency**
Users can see:

* Where money went
* Project updates
* Reward history
